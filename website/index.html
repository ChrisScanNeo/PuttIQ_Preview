How It Works

Three simple steps to improve your putting consistency

1

Choose Your Rhythm

Select your preferred BPM (70-80) and sound type. The app displays a visual timing bar that moves in sync with audio beats.

2

Enable Listen Mode

Turn on the microphone detection feature. The app will listen for your putter strike after the third beat.

3

Get Instant Feedback

See exactly when you struck the ball with color-coded accuracy. Perfect timing shows green, helping you dial in your tempo.
